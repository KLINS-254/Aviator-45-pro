Follow these simple steps to buy goods using M-Pesa

Always confirm the Phone number and amount before completing the transaction. (Amount: Sh. 975, Till Number: 6936557)
  1. Go to M-Pesa menu
    Open your M-Pesa menu on your phone
  2. Select "Lipa na M-Pesa"
    Choose "Buy Goods and Services" option
  3. Enter Till Number: "6936557"
    Input the merchant's Till number "6936557"
  4. Enter Amount and Confirm
    Input the payment amount, enter your M-Pesa PIN, and confirm the transaction
  5. Keep Confirmation SMS
    You'll receive a confirmation SMS. Keep this as proof of payment.
  6. Paste the Confirmation Code
    Paste the confirmation code you received to unlock the code.
