Payment Confirmation

Follow these simple steps to buy goods using M-Pesa

Always confirm the till number and amount before completing the transaction. (Amount: Ksh 475, Till Number: 4523484)

  1. 1

    Go to **M-Pesa** menu

    Open your M-Pesa menu on your phone

  2. 2

    Select "**Buy Goods and Services**" option

    Choose "Buy Goods and Services" option

  3. 3

    Enter **Till Number** 4523484

    Input the merchant's till number 4523484

  4. 4

    Enter **Amount and Confirm**

    Input the payment amount, enter your M-Pesa PIN, and confirm the transaction

  5. 5

    Keep **Confirmation SMS**

    You'll receive a confirmation SMS. Keep this as proof of payment.

  6. 6

    Paste the **Confirmation Code**

    Paste the confirmation code you received to unlock the code.
